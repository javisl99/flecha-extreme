KITESURF Lessons


In our center, you will be able to take the best clases that exists. Be aware, we work at El Portil (Huelva) a place where when there is low tide, natural lakes appear, turning Kiteboarding into a kids game. Shallow clean water, without waves...A real learning track.


Our way of teaching is quit simple: we have sessions of 3 hours 60€. Quit simple!.



TERMS


Our lessons are structured in 3 hours each day.
The completion of our lessons is subject to meteorology. If there are no favorable conditions, they are deferred to the next day.
We do not charge anything in advance. The lessons are paid for each day enjoyed once the day finished.
Groups are reduced. One instructor for each student in the water.

Our lessons include:

  • Rescue boat
  • Insurance
  • Last generation equipment, wetsuit, helmet and lifejacket
