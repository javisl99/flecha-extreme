KITESURF-KAYAK-SUP-SURF-CAMPS


Do you want to learn KITESURFING with the best conditions that exist? In our kite school in Huelva we are going to put it very easy.

Our Kitesurfing courses are taught in a unbeatable enviroment (La Flecha de El Rompido), without aglomerations and with conditions to learn that will surprise you.

Get away from crowded beached, with big waves and strong currents . The main challenge of our school is that you learn to the maximum with our courses of kitesurfing.

Our kitesurf instructors, graduated from IKO (International Kiteboarding Organization) and our latest generation equipement, will make your learning safe and comfortable. DO not think about it any more and come to FLECHA EXTREME KITESURF.

In addition, in our school, you can do KAYAKING TOURS all along the coast of Huelva. Enjoy our natural landscapes and spend an unforgettable day. Organize a tour to your choice, groups, we love that you propose us things...!
And if you have children, do not forget that we have SAILING COURSES-OPTIMIST to get started in the world of sailing, CAMPS AND NATURE ENVIROMENT where they can practice kayaking, surfing, beach volley, archery, beach soccer...they will enjoy it a lot!

In our school you sail, surf, laugh and have a great time!



Our Paradise

In Flecha Extreme, we have the best Kitesurfing spot that may exist. Of that, we have no doubt!.
Imagine a place of calm and shallow water, where at all times, you will have the possibility to put on foot. We take away the waves so that your learning is simple and you can progress quickly. Experienced instructors and latest generation equipement= fast and safe improvement.
