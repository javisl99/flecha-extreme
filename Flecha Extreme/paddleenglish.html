Courses & amp; Rent.PADDLE SUP

You'll be amazed how quickly you can learn Paddle SUP in our wonderful surroundings. Choose the way you want to learn and do it at your own pace. Stand Up Paddle or Paddle Surf is a sport of growing acceptance because of its ease of learning. You will surprise yourself with the progress you will make in just a few hours.




Our experienced monitors will teach you all the secrets of this sport. We will teach you to choose the material, to know the weather conditions, the technique of paddling, your position in the board ... Crossings, waves, you decide, the important thing is to have fun! You will not find a better place to learn Paddle SUP.

How do we organize our courses?

Very easy. Modules 2 hours each for 30 € . Not everyone learns at the same pace, so make the modules you need or want. Also in our school we make it very easy: we rent the equipment for only 10 € / hour.
